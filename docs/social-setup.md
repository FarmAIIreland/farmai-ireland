## Platform Notes

### X (Twitter) — @FarmAI_Ireland
- **Goal:** Discovery and credibility — get Irish farmers, agri media, and sponsors to find FarmAI Ireland organically
- **Approach:** Brand account, not personal. John does not appear by name
- **Cadence:** Max 3 tweets/week at launch
  - **Type 1 — Article Tweet:** Auto-generated by pipeline, queued in `/docs/twitter-queue.md`. Copy-paste, 2 mins per post
  - **Type 2 — Reactive:** When significant agri news breaks. Human-written or pipeline-assisted. Not mandatory
  - **Type 3 — Engagement:** @mentions of target media accounts when genuinely relevant. Never automated
- **Follow list:** `/docs/twitter-follow-list.md` — work through tiers over first 2 weeks
- **NOT doing (yet):** No polls, threads, hot takes, paid promotion, full automation (until X API funded at $100/mo)
- **Monetisation bridge:** Twitter is top-of-funnel for sponsorship strategy. Every tweet pushes toward the newsletter

### Facebook Page
- Primary use: reach farmers aged 35–60 who aren't on X
- Share each article with a one-line hook — not the headline verbatim
- Consider boosting posts to Irish farming interests for €5–10 once the page has 50+ likes
- Join Irish farming groups and share relevant articles as genuine answers to questions being asked (not promotional)

### LinkedIn Page
- Primary use: B2B visibility — sponsors, media, co-ops, industry contacts
- Tone: slightly more formal than the site, but still plain English
- Share articles framed for the agri-business reader, not just the farmer

### YouTube
- **Pipeline**: Remotion (open-source, code-driven) + ElevenLabs TTS + Claude API script generation — fully automated, free
- **Format**: 60–90 second text-card videos, Economist-style, no AI avatar — see `/docs/youtube-brand-rules.md`
- **Prep plan**: 4-week build during Google Workspace 30-day wait (Weeks 1–4 of April)
- Subscribe target: 1,000 for YouTube Partner Programme
- Channel description: "Plain English AI guides for Irish farmers. New videos weekly. Independent. Free. All guides also at farmai.ie"
- HeyGen + Pictory deferred — Remotion is free and lives in the repo

### Instagram
- Lowest priority — repurpose existing content rather than creating native posts
- Use Canva to reformat article thumbnails as square/portrait posts
- Stories: share articles via link sticker once you have 200+ followers

### TikTok — Deferred to Month 6
- Revisit at Month 6 once YouTube is established
- If pursuing TikTok: repurpose YouTube Shorts (vertical crop) rather than native creation
- Handle to register when ready: @farmaiiireland (three i's — FarmAI + Ireland)
