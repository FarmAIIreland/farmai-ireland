## About Section

Paste this directly into the LinkedIn About field:

> FarmAI Ireland is an independent platform that explains artificial intelligence to Irish farmers in plain English — what works, what doesn't, and what's actually worth your time.
>
> We don't cover farming news. We cover how AI fits into your farming life.
>
> Every guide is free, cites public sources like Teagasc and the Department of Agriculture, and is written for farmers who are time-poor and sceptical of hype. If it doesn't save you time, money, or headache, we say so.
>
> We're not farmers. We're ambassadors and enablers — people living in rural Ireland who got frustrated that nobody was explaining AI properly to the communities that could benefit most. So we built this.
>
> Topics we cover: BISS and ACRES scheme applications, livestock health monitoring, grass measurement tools, weather and satellite data, and how to use ChatGPT without losing your mind.
>
> Visit farmai.ie for free guides, or subscribe to our monthly newsletter.
